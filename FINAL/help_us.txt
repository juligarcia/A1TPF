*** HELP ***
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Program's arguments
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Arg.	Option	Description
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-h		Shows help
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-m	N	Simpletron has a memory of N size. By omission, N equals 50.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-	stdin	The program will be read from stdin.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
F_name	Entry	Entry files should be entered on an organized manner after the rest of the args.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-of	bin	The dump will be in binary form.
	txt	The dump will be in text form.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
